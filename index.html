ROXZY PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy describes how Neporrex ("Developer") collects, uses, and protects personal information obtained through the use of Roxzy ("Bot"). By using the Bot, you consent to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using Roxzy.

    Information Collection


User-Provided Information: When you interact with Roxzy, the Developer may collect certain personal information, including but not limited to Discord user IDs, server IDs, and preferences set through bot commands related to teammate search (e.g., game names, skill levels, regions).

Usage Information: The Developer may collect information about your interactions with Roxzy, including commands used, server activity, and other usage data. This data helps improve bot features and the teammate-matching experience.

    Use of Information


The Developer uses the collected information for the following purposes:

- to provide and maintain Roxzy’s teammate search functionality;
- to respond to support requests and improve user experience;
- to match users with relevant teammates based on submitted criteria;
- to analyze usage trends and improve the bot’s efficiency and security;
- to comply with legal obligations and protect the rights of users and the Developer.

The Developer does not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. Anonymous or aggregated data may be shared with third-party services to improve performance or for analytics purposes.

    Data Storage and Security


The Developer takes reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is fully secure, and risks still exist with any online platform.

Information is stored on secure servers or trusted platforms. By using Roxzy, you consent to this information being stored and processed in different regions as necessary.

    Data Retention


Personal information will be retained only for as long as necessary to provide the services described. Users may request data deletion at any time, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

    Third-Party Links and Services


Roxzy may include links to external platforms or services. The Developer is not responsible for their privacy practices, and users should consult their policies separately.

    Children's Privacy


Roxzy is intended for users aged 13 or older. No data is knowingly collected from children under this age. Any such data discovered will be promptly deleted.

    Changes to the Privacy Policy


The Developer may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of Roxzy following updates indicates acceptance of the new terms. Users are encouraged to check this page regularly.
